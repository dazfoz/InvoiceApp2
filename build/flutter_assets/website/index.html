Easy Invoice Creation

Creating invoices is simple and intuitive with Bolt's user-friendly interface.

  • Add line items with descriptions, quantities, and prices
  • Automatic tax calculation based on your settings
  • Add notes and payment terms for your clients
  • Preview invoices before finalizing

Comprehensive Client Management

Keep all your client information organized and accessible.

  • Store client contact details and addresses
  • View invoice history for each client
  • Track outstanding balances
  • Add notes and custom fields for better organization

Professional PDF Invoices

Generate beautiful, professional PDF invoices that impress your clients.

  • Customizable invoice templates
  • Include your company logo and branding
  • Detailed line items and payment information
  • Share directly via email or download
